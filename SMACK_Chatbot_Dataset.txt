Q: What does SMACK stand for?

Q: Where can I access SMACK?
A: You can access SMACK at: https://Vishnuat18.github.io/home.html

SECTION 2: AUTHENTICATION
================================================================

Q: How do I create an account on SMACK?
A: On the login page (index.html), click "Create one" under the login form. Fill in your Full Name, Email, Password, Confirm Password, and solve the CAPTCHA verification. Then click "Sign Up."

Q: How do I log in?
A: Go to the SMACK login page, enter your registered email and password, and click "Login."

Q: What happens if I enter the wrong CAPTCHA?
A: You will see an "Incorrect Captcha!" alert and a new CAPTCHA will be generated automatically.

Q: Can I refresh the CAPTCHA?
A: Yes, there is a refresh button next to the CAPTCHA display on the sign-up form.

Q: What should I do if my passwords don't match during sign-up?
A: You will see a "Passwords do not match!" alert. Re-enter both password fields to match.

Q: How do I log out?
A: Click the logout icon (sign-out arrow) in the navigation bar, or use the "Sign Out from SMACK" button on your Profile page.

Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?
A: Currently, the portal uses Firebase Authentication. You can use the Firebase password reset flow. Contact the admin at vishnurajan24766@gmail.com for assistance.

Q: Can anyone sign up or is it restricted?
A: Anyone with an email address can sign up. However, admin access is restricted to the portal admin only.

SECTION 3: NAVIGATION & PAGES

Q: What pages does SMACK have?
A: SMACK has the following pages:

   - home.html — Main dashboard with subject cards

A: The Home page shows a greeting, an upcoming event countdown, a search bar, and cards for all 6 subjects. Clicking a subject card takes you to that subject's materials page.

Q: How do I go back to the Home page from any page?
A: Click the left arrow (back button) in the top header, or click "Home" in the navigation bar, or press the Home icon in the mobile bottom navigation.

Q: What does the upcoming event section show?
A: It shows the next important academic event (e.g., an upcoming exam) with the date and subject name. For example: "28/04/2026: OOSE Exam."

Q: How does the search bar work?
A: Type at least 2 characters in the search bar on the Home page. It searches across all subjects and materials by name. Matching PDF files appear inline below the search bar. Click any result to open it in the viewer.

SECTION 9: TECHNICAL & FIREBASE BACKEND
================================================================

Q: What technology powers SMACK?
A: SMACK is a static web app built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It uses Firebase for:
   - Firebase Authentication — User login and sign-up
   - Firebase Firestore — User profiles, feedback storage, subject data
   - Firebase Realtime Database — Supplementary data
   - Firebase Analytics — Usage tracking

Q: Is my data safe on SMACK?
A: SMACK uses Firebase Firestore Security Rules:
   - Users can only read/write their own profile data.
   - Only admins can write study materials or read all feedback.
   - Any authenticated user can submit feedback.
   - All other documents are denied by default.

Q: What happens when I sign up — how is my account created?
A: When you sign up, Firebase Authentication creates your account and Firestore saves your profile (name, email, role, creation date). The admin email (vishnurajan24766@gmail.com) is automatically assigned the "admin" role.

Q: Does SMACK use any external libraries?
A: Yes, SMACK uses:
   - Font Awesome 6.4.0 — Icons
   - Google Fonts (Outfit, Plus Jakarta Sans) — Typography
   - PDF.js 3.4.120 — In-browser PDF rendering
   - Firebase 12.11.0 — Backend services
